Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Introduction

EchoTrader.ai ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

By using EchoTrader.ai, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, password
  • Profile Information: Bio, avatar, website, trading preferences, investment profile
  • Portfolio Data: Holdings, transactions, performance data you manually enter
  • Brokerage Credentials: Encrypted credentials for brokerage account integration (e.g., Interactive Brokers)
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed through Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Communications: Messages, posts, comments, and other content you create

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent, clicks, searches
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (not precise GPS)
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, analytics cookies, preference cookies

1.3 AI and Chat Data

  • Chat Logs: Conversations with our AI assistant to improve responses
  • Signal Interactions: Signals you view, trade, save, or dismiss
  • Preferences: Your feedback, ratings, and customization choices

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Provide and Improve the Service

  • Deliver personalized AI insights and teaching signals
  • Track your portfolio performance and generate analytics
  • Sync data with your connected brokerage accounts
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our algorithms, AI models, and user experience

2.2 Communications

  • Send transactional emails (account confirmations, password resets)
  • Deliver service updates and important notices
  • Send marketing communications (opt-in only, unsubscribe anytime)
  • Notify you of new features or promotions

2.3 Safety and Security

  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and policies
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests
  • Protect the rights and safety of our users

3. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us:

  • Cloud Hosting: Google Cloud Platform, Firebase
  • Payment Processing: Stripe
  • AI Services: Google Gemini AI Studio
  • Market Data: Financial data providers
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymized)
  • Email Services: Email delivery providers

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for employees
  • Secure Storage: Data stored on SOC 2 compliant cloud infrastructure
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, PDPA), you may have the following rights:

  • Access and Portability: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Restriction: Restrict or object to certain data processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise your rights, contact: privacy@echotrader.ai

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Session management and authentication
  • Analytics Cookies: Usage statistics and performance monitoring
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Track conversions (opt-in only)

7. Contact Us

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy:

  • Email: privacy@echotrader.ai
